🔑 Book Immediately

12:00 PM
🍽 Lunch

Low-key gem with phenomenal food. Pork belly sandwich + loaded tater tots are must-orders. Friendly staff, great vibe, exactly the kind of spot that makes a road trip. Very reasonable prices.
202 W Broadway St · Open 11 AM–10 PM daily
Or
🍽 Alt Lunch
Bourbon On Main · Frankfort
River-view patio, bourbon-forward menu, Black & Bleu Burger is legendary. Best patio views of the Kentucky River. Great for groups.
103 W Main St · Open 11 AM daily
